Map Tool Overview

Take a moment to read over the following information. In the next part of this survey you will be mapping your last trip.

First, you will be presented with a map of the Massachusetts coast.
Image of map controls goes here
  • Navigation controls are provided allowing you to 'move' the map and 'zoom' in to the starting point of your last trip
Next, you will draw a line on the map representing the route of your last trip from start to finish.
Image of drawn route goes here
  • Draw it from start to finish even if you returned along the same route.
  • Just be as accurate as you can.
Finally, you will be asked to draw areas along that route where you particpated in different activities.
Image of areas drawn along route
  • Draw each activity area one at a time
  • For each area you'll answer a few questions about why you choose to recreate there.

If you need additional help, refer to our frequently asked questions page. You can also email help@maboatersurvey.com.
