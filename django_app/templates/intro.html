Welcome to the mapping part of the survey.

You just finished answering questions about a specific trip you took out on the water. We would now like you to plot the approximate route you took and tell us what you did during that trip.

Instructions: 3 Steps Below

  • Step 1: Zoom in on the map to where you started your last trip. Map navigation controls will be provided for this purpose.
  • Step 2: Plot the route you took during your last trip. Please plot the COMPLETE trip even if you followed the same route for your return leg.
  • Step 3: Plot the points where you engaged in particular recreational activities during this trip.

If you need additional help, please keep your mapping application open and either:

1) call 617-737-2600 ext. 102,
2) email help@seaplan.org,
3) refer to our detailed drawing instructions page, or
4) view our tutorial video.

Note: If you exit the mapping application, you will not be able to re-enter the survey.
