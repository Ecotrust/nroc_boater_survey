Please take a minute to read the following instructions as they will explain what we would like you to do.

Welcome to the mapping part of the survey. You just finished answering questions about a specific trip you took out on the water. We would now like you to plot the approximate route that you took and tell us what you did during that trip.

  • First, you will be asked to zoom in on the map to where you started your last trip. Map navigation controls will be provided for this purpose.
  • You will then be asked to plot the route you took during your last trip. Please plot the COMPLETE trip even if you followed the same route for your return leg.
  • Finally, you will be asked to draw the areas where you engaged in particular recreational activities during this trip.

If you need additional help, please keep your mapping application open and either:

  1. call 617-737-2600 ext. 102
  2. email kstarbuck@seaplan.org
  3. refer to our detailed drawing instructions page, or
  4. view our tutorial video.

Important note: If you exit the mapping application, you will not be able to re-enter the survey.
